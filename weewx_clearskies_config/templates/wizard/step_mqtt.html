How to set up MQTT

MQTT requires three things:

  1. A message broker (like EMQX or Mosquitto) running on your network
  2. weewx configured to publish — add the weewx-mqtt extension to your weewx installation and configure it to publish loop packets to the broker
  3. The connection details below — enter your broker's address, credentials, and the topic weewx publishes to

If you already have weewx publishing to MQTT (e.g., for the Belchertown skin), enter the same broker and credentials below.
