Earthquake Settings

Configure the earthquake data display on the Earthquakes dashboard page. These settings control which events are shown — the data source (provider) is configured separately under Earthquakes Provider.

Filter settings Distance from your station's coordinates within which to show earthquakes. Default: 500 km. Range: 10 – 20 000 km. Only show earthquakes at or above this Richter magnitude. Default: 2.5. Range: 0 – 10. How many days of earthquake history to show by default. Default: 30 days. Range: 1 – 365 days.
